Riccardo Taormina

Riccardo Taormina is an Assistant Professor in Urban Water Infrastructure at TU Delft Department of Water Management. Before joining TU Delft, Riccardo worked as a Postdoctoral Research Fellow at the iTrust Centre for Research in Cyber Security of SUTD. He earned his PhD in Coastal and Hydraulic Engineering at the Department of Civil and Environmental Engineering at Hong Kong Polytechnic University in 2015.

Elad Salomons

Elad’s focus is on water distribution systems management, modelling, optimization, water security, software development and research.
